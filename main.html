Manali, Himachal Pradesh


Nestled in the Himalayas, Manali is a serene town known for its snow-capped peaks, lush valleys, and adventure sports. Whether you're looking to go skiing in Solang Valley, relax by the Beas River, or explore the spiritual vibes of Hadimba Temple, Manali offers something for every traveler. It's a perfect blend of natural beauty and cultural richness.

  1. Solang Valley – Famous for skiing, paragliding, and other adventure sports.
  2. Hadimba Devi Temple – A historic wooden temple surrounded by cedar forests.
  3. Rohtang Pass – Offers breathtaking snow views and a gateway to Lahaul-Spiti.
  4. Old Manali – Known for its bohemian cafes and peaceful river trails.
  5. Jogini Waterfalls – A short trek leads to this picturesque natural beauty.
  • Siddu – A steamed wheat bun with a spiced walnut filling.
  • Trout Fish – Locally sourced and perfectly grilled in Himalayan herbs.
  • Chha Gosht – A flavorful lamb curry made with gram flour and curd.
  • Babru – A Himachali version of kachori filled with black gram paste.
  • Mittha – A traditional sweet dish made with sweetened rice and dry fruits.
